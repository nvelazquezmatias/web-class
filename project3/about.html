About

Since the beginning of the pandemic, there has been a growing concern and criticism surrounding the excessive consumption of fashion.

During the pandemic, with most things taking place virtually, we witnessed a significant change in how people consume fashion. Almost every other week, a new micro-trend emerged and discarded within a year.

Shein's presence on TikTok has driven this shift in the fashion industry. With over 2 billion downloads in 2020, TikTok has become a leading platform for fashion lovers to showcase their latest Shein hauls in short, engaging videos.

Is social media marketing causing a decline in fashion quality? Influencers are the leading marketers in the industry. But with the soaring success of fast fashion brands like Shein, even high-end fashion brands have adopted this model. It makes you wonder if the focus is on quantity over quality.
